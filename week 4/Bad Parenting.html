Summary


"Bad Parenting" is a 2024 indie psychological horror game that has gained attention for its eerie atmosphere, unique storyline, and surreal visuals. The game revolves around the unsettling concept of a fictional character, "Mr. Red Face," who was originally created by parents as a tool to discipline their children, but suddenly becomes real. The game explores dark themes of family dynamics, trauma, and the consequences of bad parenting, mixing both tragedy and surrealism. It is a first-person adventure game with a strong focus on narrative, and players have praised its deeply emotional, lore-rich story and haunting, atmospheric gameplay. The game is particularly notable for its unpredictable plot twists, leaving players with a lasting emotional impact.
